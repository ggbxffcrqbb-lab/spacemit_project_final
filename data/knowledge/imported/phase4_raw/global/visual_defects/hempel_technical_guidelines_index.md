Technical Guidelines

Getting the most out of Hempel's products

Hempel has a multitude of products designed for a broad range of areas and exposure conditions. It is essential to apply these products onto a properly prepared surface using the correct application method, to achieve the expected performance of the coating system.

At Hempel, we have prepared a number of Technical guidelines to help you get the most out of our products. The documents are intended for professional use and provide generic advice. If you are in doubt or have any questions about specific details, your local Hempel representative is available to help you.
